Development Tool

Whan whan is an e-commerce website for Whan Whan Thai dessert shop which owned by an alumni of Assumtion University. We tried to keep the design simply and be easy to use. The purpose of this project are to provide the convenience for shop's owner to manage the orders and for customers of Whan Whan to see the menu and make their order through website.
